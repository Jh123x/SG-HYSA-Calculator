Eligibility

  • Age: 18 and above
  • The SaveUp Account in this programme must be linked to a Maybank Debit Card.
  • Initial deposit:
    • S$500 for Singapore Citizens and Singapore Permanent Residents.
    • S$1,000 for other nationalities.

 

TO APPLY

  • Apply online or via Maybank2u SG app (for Singapore Citizens and Singapore Permanent Residents)

Rates

  • View base interest rates.

Fees & Charges

  • S$2 monthly balance fee (if average daily balance falls below S$1,000). Waived for customers below 25 years old.
  • S$30 early account closure charge (if the account is closed within 6 months).
